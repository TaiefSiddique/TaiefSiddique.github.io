About Us

The Green University Computer Club Permanent Campus is a community of computer enthusiasts and professionals who come together to share their knowledge, collaborate on projects, and promote the use of technology for social and environmental causes.

Our mission is to create a platform where individuals can learn, grow, and make a positive impact through technology. We believe that by working together, we can create innovative solutions that address social and environmental challenges.

Our club welcomes students, faculty, staff, and alumni from Green University and other educational institutions, as well as professionals from the tech industry who are passionate about making a difference. We offer a variety of activities and events, including workshops, seminars, hackathons, and community service projects.
